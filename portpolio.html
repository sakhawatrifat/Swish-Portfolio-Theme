Brief About Swish

SWISH started its work from the background in 2012 as Halal Trade Center as an importing and sales company for sanitary ware. In 2015 Halal Trade center started its own brand by the name of SWISH which started with the vision of combining quality sanitary ware products with luxurious designs. SWISH started a revolution in this industry and so the journey to become the best in the world started.

The company has grown steadily through the years by emphasizing its philosophy of customer satisfaction and always putting the quality of the product first. Here at swish quality comes first and we have held that ideology from the very beginning.

Today, SWISH offers complete sanitary ware and kitchenware solutions with luxurious and best quality products for satisfying the needs of our valuable clients.

Growth

When SWISH started its journey way back in 2012 only a handful of people knew about us. As the years went by SWISH kept on growing and never stopped compromising with their product quality till this day. Today in 2020 SWISH has grown as a brand that is known for its quality products and customer satisfaction. SWISH is one of the very few brands in the sanitary ware industry in Bangladesh that operates with a Fixed Price policy. SWISH is now considered as one of the best sanitary ware brands that are operating in the country.

Participations

Over the years SWISH have participated in various types of exhibitions among which “Interior Exterior International Expo” which was held at International Convention City, Bashundhara, Dhaka was a notable one. SWISH aspires to participate in the future exhibitions held across the country to make its presence felt.

Teams

Sales & Marketing, Creative and Delivery these are the three teams that are currently working tirelessly for SWISH to achieve their one and only goal which is to be the best of the world. All the team members are like a family who knows how to dream big and always ready to climb the mountain ahead of them to achieve that dream.

Social Contributions

At SWISH we believe that not a single child have to choose the way of begging and education should be for everyone. So for the less fortunate children around us SWISH has started the SWISH Foundation with the for providing their education cost and their welfare. SWISH hopes to expand this foundations works all over the country for the wellbeing of these less fortunate children.

Vision

Our vision is simple, to be the very best in the world. SWISH hopes to hit the global market in the near future and to secure the global market as one of the best brand on earth.

But achieving this feat is not so simple. We have a mountain to climb in front of us, and our team is ready to climb every mountain and travel the extra mile to ensure that our dream and our ambition to become the very best is achieved.
